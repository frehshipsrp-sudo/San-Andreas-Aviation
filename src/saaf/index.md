## Overview

Welcome to the **largest subdivision within SAA** — the **San Andreas Air Force (SAAF)**.
We offer **high-quality Air Force roleplay**, including:

* Stunt Teams
* Mission Operations
* Formation Flights
* Special Transport Operations

## Our Mission

Our goal is to **grow and strengthen the Air Force community** within **Paradise State Roleplay**, making us the **only currently active Air Force in PSRP**.

## Why Join SAAF?

* **Active High Command** always available to assist with questions or issues
* **Highly active pilots**, ensuring fun and engaging roleplay at all times
* **The only Air Force division in Paradise State Roleplay**, with much more content coming soon

### Requirements to Join

* Must hold the rank of **Private Pilot** or higher

### If you would like to apply for SAAF, click the link below!

[Apply for SAAF!](https://discord.com/channels/1083112361428258887/1195157472877482054)

### Training Process

Once accepted, all recruits must complete **mandatory Air Force training**.
After successfully completing training, you will officially join **SAAF**.
